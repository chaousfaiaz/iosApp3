iOSApp3 – Scavenger Hunt App
Your city’s Chamber of Commerce wants you to create a scavenger hunt app that provides discounts and prizes to promote local businesses that wish to participate (restaurants, movie theatres, book stores, etc.).
There are 10 items hidden at various local businesses, and the app should provide clues for each one. When a participant locates an item, they must take a picture of it in the app. When all items have been found, 
they can submit their results online. If they successfully found 5 or more items, they’ll receive a 10% discount code. If they successfully found 7 or more items, they’ll instead receive a 20% discount code. 
And if they successfully found all items, they’ll also be entered into a draw for a $5000 grand prize
Features:

List of 10 businesses with clues
Card flip to photo submission
Progress counter + reward tiers
Simple Map integration
Assignment 4 will expand this with Location API, persistence, and polish.
